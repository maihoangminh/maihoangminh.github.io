International students in the United States by the number

United States has the world’s largest international student population, with over 1,000,000 students choosing to broaden their education and life experience.

By Minh Mai

Why United States?

Over 5% of all students enrolled in higher-level education in the USA are international students, and the numbers are growing. From the mid-1950’s, when international student enrollment was only just reaching 35,000, international education in the USA has come a long way.

Over 4,900 colleges, universities and training institutes in the United States offer undergraduate and graduate courses in more than 900 different specialized expertises. Tight relationship between teaching and research in the field to enhance further its position and the quality of education in America.

Every year, the US has always had a number of colleges ranked in the list of top universities in the world. Times Higher Education releases ranked universities in the world and according to the data

Total number by year

The number of international students have been increasing gradually at the rate of 5-10% in the recent years, peaking at over 1 million student at the moment. The number will be anticipated to increase at the same rate in the future.

Where are they from

Data by November 2016

China is currently having the most foreign students in America, followed by India and Saudi Arabia. Students from the three countries represent half of the total number of international students in the world.

Vietnam eventhough is a small country in area and population but have a relatively high number of international students studying in United States. Vietnam has over 30 thousand students who are studying in United States.

International students population by states (November 2016)

Data by November 2016

Looking at the map, we can see that the international student are concerntrated in the states that have more metropolitan areas.

The number of foreign students in California was 10.5% higher than the previous academic year. Four of the top 20 institutions that are having the most international students are in the state of California.

New York State is also a popular study destination, with New York University topping all schools with 15,543 foreign students, and Columbia University is currently hosting 12,740 international students.
